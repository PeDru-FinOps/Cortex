## **Alert Criteria**  

Green: Access to port 80, 25, 443, or 465 is unrestricted.  
Red: Access to port 20, 21, 1433, 1434, 3306, 3389, 4333, 5432, or 5500 is unrestricted.  
Yellow: Access to any other port is unrestricted.
